ABOUT SPLASH!

Water is an essntial for everyday life, and like many of earths natural resources it is limited. Only two percent point five of the water on earth suitable for drinking , and with our ever growing population it seems only logical to consevere this precious recourse. Water conservation is the process of using water sparingly. only using enough to get by ,not in execive amounts . Conserving water minimizes the effects of drought and water shortages. By reducing the amount of water we use now we can help. Splash aims to help to educate indiviuals on how to contribute towards a solution on our water crisis.
